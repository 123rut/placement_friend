# Product Foundation

## Users

- B.Tech students
- MCA students
- Final-year students
- Internship and placement seekers

## Core Problems

- Opportunity information is fragmented across company sites, college portals, and informal groups.
- Students often see openings after deadlines or after eligibility windows close.
- Generic alert channels create noise and reduce trust.

## MVP Jobs To Be Done

1. Let a student sign up with a college email.
2. Capture a reliable profile with branch, CGPA, batch year, and preferred companies.
3. Track selected company career pages on a schedule.
4. Extract new opportunities and remove duplicates.
5. Match opportunities to student eligibility.
6. Notify only qualified students quickly.
7. Show upcoming opportunities in a simple dashboard.

## Product Principles

- Mobile-first interaction
- Minimal UI and fast onboarding
- No spam notifications
- No duplicate opportunities
- High reliability around deadlines

## Non-Goals For MVP

- Community features
- Public discussion feeds
- AI recommendations beyond rule-based matching
- Broad social scraping across every channel from day one
